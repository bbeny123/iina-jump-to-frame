You can toggle the Jump to Frame panel via:
  • Menu: Plugin → Jump...
  • Keybind: ⌘ + G (customizable via Preferences)
  • Keybind conflict validation requires IINA restart and media load
  • Some binds may fail silently while appearing "Valid" in Preferences
The input is flexible. You can enter:
  • Frame number: e.g., 1500
  • Timecode: e.g., 1:20 or 00:01:20.500
